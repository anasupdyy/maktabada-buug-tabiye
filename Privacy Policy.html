Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: May 29, 2025

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, including our use of Google AdSense.

1. Information Collection

We collect information to provide better services to our users:

  • Personal Data: Name, email when you contact us
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device

2. How We Use Information

Your information helps us:

  • Improve website content and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Display personalized content and ads (via Google AdSense)
  • Analyze website traffic patterns

3. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display relevant ads. Google uses cookies to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to our site
  • Collect aggregated data about ad performance

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Data Protection

We implement security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

5. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified via email or website notice.
